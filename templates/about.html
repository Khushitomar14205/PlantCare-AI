Advanced Plant Disease Detection Using Transfer Learning

üìã Project Overview

PlantCare AI is an advanced machine learning project designed to detect and classify plant diseases using deep learning techniques. The annual worldwide crop loss is estimated at USD 220 billion due to plant diseases, making early detection crucial for agricultural sustainability.

This project utilizes MobileNetV2, a pre-trained convolutional neural network (CNN), with transfer learning to accurately classify plant diseases. The model has been trained on the New Plant Diseases Dataset containing over 87,000 annotated plant leaf images across 38 distinct classes.

Our system can identify diseases affecting major crops including Apple, Corn, Grape, Potato, Tomato, and more, helping farmers and gardeners take timely action to protect their plants and maximize yields.
